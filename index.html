Background Information

We are Team 17, consisting of 4 members: Huseyn Ibrahimov, Murad Abbasov, Nazrin Gadirli, and Jala Garayeva. We are excited to share our journey together!

Starting off with the first project, building various gates on the breadboard, we passionately learned many things about working together.

Afterwards, utilizing the experience that we had gained, we organized our Hour of Code event in the Technical-Humanitarian Lyceum.

As some of the equipment were not available, we decided to adapt to those conditions and used the provided laptop with the projected screen
to showcase interactive tasks that were freely accessible on Code.org website.

It was very insightful and unique interactions were also quite enjoyable.
